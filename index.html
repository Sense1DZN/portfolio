About Me

My name is Gabriel or “Sense1” and I'm a 16 year old graphic designer focused on esports, branding, thumbnails, and occasionally logos. I specialize in creating original, jaw-dropping designs that are clean to look at in composition while still standing out from other designs. I started gaming when I was 9 years old, and eventually, with the purchase of a gaming setup in 2019, I began streaming on Twitch. When I realized that I wasn't consistent with my Twitch streams, I moved to an area I felt fit me perfectly: graphic design. Ever since September of 2021, I have consistently increased my skill and maintained a level of professionalism, quality, and originality that helps me stand out in the community.
